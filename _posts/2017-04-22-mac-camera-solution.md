## Solution 1

1. Open your terminal and input `sudo killall VDCAssistant`;
2. Enter your password and quit terminal.

## Solution 2

1. Open your Activity Monitor;
2. Search `VDCAssistant`;
3. Choose all related threads and force quit them.
